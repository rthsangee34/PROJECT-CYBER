THE PSYCHOLOGY

Attackers use Emotional Hijacking. By creating a crisis (Urgency/Fear), they force the victim's brain to switch from "Analytical Mode" to "Survival Mode," making them overlook obvious technical errors.

THE TECHNICAL

The "HTTPS Lock" icon only means the connection is encrypted, NOT that the site is legitimate. Attackers easily obtain free SSL certificates to make their fake sites look "Secure."
