What is Online Fraud?
Online fraud refers to any type of scheme that uses the internet to trick victims out of money, property, or sensitive data.

Common Scams:
This includes fake shopping sites, lottery "wins", investment scams, and impersonation of family members in trouble.

🛠️ Anti-Fraud Tips

  • • Too Good to be True: If a prize or investment seems perfect, it's likely a scam.
  • • Check URLs: Fraudulent sites often have typos (e.g., 'amozon.com').
  • • Secure Payment: Only shop on sites with 'https' and valid SSL certificates.
  • • No OTP Sharing: Never share your OTP with anyone, even 'bank staff'.
