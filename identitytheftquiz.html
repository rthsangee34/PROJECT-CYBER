What is Identity Theft?
Identity theft occurs when someone steals your personal information—like your ID, bank details, or passwords—to commit fraud or other crimes in your name.

The Impact:
Criminals can open bank accounts, take out loans, or even commit crimes while pretending to be you, leading to severe financial and legal damage.

🛡️ Identity Protection Tips

  • • Document Shredding: Always shred physical bank statements and ID copies.
  • • MFA is Key: Enable Multi-Factor Authentication on all social and bank accounts.
  • • Monitor Reports: Regularly check your credit report for unknown accounts.
  • • Social Privacy: Don't share your full name, birthdate, or address publicly.
