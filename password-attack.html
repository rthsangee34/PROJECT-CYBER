The Math of Defense

Adding just ONE character of a different type (symbol, number) increases the Entropy exponentially. A 12-character random string takes trillions of years to brute-force with current hardware.

Modern Cracking

Hackers rarely start with "aaaaa". They use Rainbow Tables and leaked Dictionaries from massive data breaches. If your password is in their list, complexity doesn't matter.
