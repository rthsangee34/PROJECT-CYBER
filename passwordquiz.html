Password Attack Defense

What are Password Attacks?
Password attacks are various techniques used by cybercriminals to gain unauthorized access to accounts by cracking or stealing passwords.

Cracking vs Stealing:
Attackers use powerful computers to guess thousands of passwords (Brute Force) or trick you into entering them on fake sites (Phishing).

🔐 Password Best Practices

  • • Uniqueness: Use a different password for every single account.
  • • MFA: Enable Multi-Factor Authentication everywhere you can.
  • • Manager: Use a reputable Password Manager to store complex passwords.
  • • Complexity: Minimum 12 characters, mix of upper/lower, numbers, and symbols.
