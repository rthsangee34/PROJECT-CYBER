Social Engineering Defense

What is Social Engineering?
It is the art of manipulating people so they give up confidential information. Unlike traditional hacking, attackers exploit human psychology rather than technical flaws.

Common Tactics:
Attackers use urgency, fear, or fake authority to trick you into clicking links, sharing passwords, or wire transferring money.

🛠️ Social Engineering Prevention

  • • Slow Down: Social engineers want you to act in a panic.
  • • Verify Identity: Call back numbers from official websites, not DMs.
  • • Reject Assistance: Be wary of unsolicited tech support offers.
  • • No Sharing: A bank will never ask for your PIN via phone or email.
