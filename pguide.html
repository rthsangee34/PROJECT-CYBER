What is Phishing?

Phishing is a digital trick where attackers disguise themselves as a trustworthy entity (like your bank, Netflix, or even your school) to steal your sensitive data.

🔑 Passwords
💳 Credit Cards
🛡️ Identity

"If it sounds too good to be true, or too scary to ignore, it's probably phishing."

Attack Vectors

Email

Email Phishing

Explanation :- The most common form. Massive amounts of emails sent to thousands of people hoping someone clicks.


"Your bank account will be blocked,Click here to verify".

SMS

Smishing

Explaintion :- Urgent text messages about "unclaimed packages" or "account blocks" containing malicious links.


Example :- "You won the prize! Click this link to claim".

Targeted

Spear Phishing

Explanation :- A surgical strike. The attacker researches you first to make the email look extremely personal and real.


Example :- A student receives an email that looks like it's from their school principal asking for longin details.

Voice

Vishing

Explanation :- Phone calls using social engineering to trick you into revealing an OTP or remote access to your PC.


Example :- Someone calls pretending to be from the banks and asks your OTP.

SOCIAL MEDIA PHISHING

SOCIAL MEDIA

Explanation :- Fake massages or login pages on social media.


Example :- A fake login page that looks like Instagram asking you sign in again.

CLONE PHISHING

CLONE PHISHING

Explanation :- A real email copied and changed with a fake link.


Example :- You receive an email that looks exactly like a previous real massag,but the link is replaced.

SIMPLE MEANING

SIMPLE MEANING

Explanation :- ALL THESE PHISHING TYPES TRY TO TRICK PEOPLE INTO GIVING PERSONAL INFORMATION.

Realistic Examples

Example 1) Fake Bank Mail 📧

Subject: Urgent! your account will be blocked

Dear Customer,
We detected unusual activity in your bank account please click the link below to verify immediately❗.

Example URL : www.secure-verufy.com

    🔎Red Flags❗
  • 🔹 Urgent Warning!
  • 🔹 Strange Website Links
  • 🔹 Generic Greeting
Example No.2) Fake Social Media Mail 📧

Subject : Your account has been suspended.

Explanation :- Urgent text messages about "unclaimed packages" or "account blocks" containing malicious links.

Example :- "You won the prize! Click this link to claim".

Targeted

Spear Phishing

Hello,

    🔎Red Flags❗

Subject : Congratulation! You won.

  • 🔹 Too good to be true.

CYBER SECURITY BASIC TO PROTECT YOURSELF FROM PHISHING 🔎

If you receive fake emails, messages, or calls, report them immediately to stay safe.

Don't click unknown links 🔗

If you get a strange SMS🎯 or EMAIL📧,don't click the link❗.

Check the sender carefully 👁️

Make sure the email 📧 is from a real company, not a fake address🎯.

Never share passwords or OTP

Banks and Companies will never ask your password or OTP.

Check the website link 🔗

Make sure the website has HTTPS and correct name.

Example : Real -> google.com✅
Fake -> login123.com❎

Don't trust urgent messages ❎

Phishing messages say things like:
"Your account will be blocked! Click now!".

There is a trick ❗

Enable Two Factor Authentication (2FA) ✅

This adds extra protection to your account. 🤳

Think before click 🤔

If something looks suspicious, don't click it❗.
