SIM Swap Alert

What is SIM Swap Fraud?
It is a type of account takeover fraud where a scammer tricks your mobile carrier into transferring your phone number to a SIM card they control.

The Danger:
Once they have your number, they receive your SMS OTPs (One-Time Passwords) for your bank, email, and social media, allowing them to bypass security and steal your money or data.

🛡️ SIM Protection Tips

  • • Carrier PIN: Set up a porting-out PIN or passcode with your mobile provider.
  • • No SMS 2FA: Use authentication apps (Google Auth) instead of SMS OTPs where possible.
  • • Signal Loss: If your phone suddenly loses signal while others have it, contact your carrier immediately.
  • • Social Privacy: Don't link your phone number to public social media profiles.
