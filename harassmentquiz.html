What is Online Harassment?
It is the use of digital platforms to harass, intimidate, or bully individuals. This includes spreading rumors, doxxing, impersonation, and sending abusive messages.

Global Impact:
Cyberbullying leads to severe emotional distress and can happen to anyone, anywhere. Awareness and knowing how to block and report are the best defenses.

🛡️ Online Safety Tips

  • • Block & Report: Don't engage with bullies. Use platform tools to block them immediately.
  • • Evidence: Take screenshots of harassment for reporting to authorities.
  • • Privacy Settings: Keep your social media profiles private and limit who can message you.
  • • Digital Footprint: Think carefully before posting personal info that could be used for doxxing.
