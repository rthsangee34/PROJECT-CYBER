How it works

1. Information Gathering: Attackers find victim details (SSN, Phone, DOB) via data breaches or phishing.

2. The Social Engineering: By calling support centers and impersonating the victim, they convince agents to swap the SIM card.

3. Identity Theft: Once the phone number is ported, the attacker triggers password resets for bank and social media accounts.

Prevention

  • Enable a Transfer Lock or PIN on your cellular account.
  • Avoid SMS-based 2FA; use app-based authenticators (Authy, Google Auth).
  • Be wary of social engineering phishing calls from "support."
