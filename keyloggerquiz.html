What is a Keylogger?
A keylogger is a type of surveillance software or hardware that records every keystroke you type on your computer or mobile device.

The Invisible Thief:
Attackers use them to steal passwords, bank details, and private conversations without you ever noticing they are there.

🛡️ Keylogger Prevention Tips

  • • Antivirus: Use reputable anti-malware and keep it updated.
  • • No Public PCs: Never access sensitive info (banking) on public computers.
  • • Virtual Keyboard: Use on-screen keyboards for passwords when on suspicious devices.
  • • Software Integrity: Only download programs from official, verified sources.
